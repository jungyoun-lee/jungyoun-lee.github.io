Jung Youn Lee

I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at Rice University, Jones Graduate School of Business. My research interests lie broadly in the social and distributional consequences of firm and government policy, with a particular interest in the consumer credit market. I use data from various domains to understand how consumer data, fairness constraints, and privacy preferences shape credit market outcomes.
